    mobile conference

    Annual International Mobile conference will be held on May 15th in Digital October Center in Moscow.


    The main topic of the conference is the usage of mobile technologies in different spheres of business.

    The goal of #MBLT is to provide a view on current mobile technologies that are changing our everyday lives and influence on the behavior and thinking of potential customers.

    Visionaries and expert practitioners will talk about mobile business cases from different spheres: medicine, sport, music, security, banking and other spheres that are interesting for the global and Russian markets.
